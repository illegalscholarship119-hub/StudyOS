# 📚 StudyOS

> Your personal AI-powered study workspace.

---

# 🎯 Goal

StudyOS helps students organize everything related to studying in one place.

Instead of keeping notes in YouTube, ChatGPT, Claude, PDFs, screenshots and notebooks...

Everything lives inside one topic.

---

# Features

- Screenshot Saving
- Paste Text
- PDF Upload
- YouTube Clips
- Personal Notes
- AI Summary
- AI Question Generator
- AI Flashcards
- AI Chat

---

- Settings
